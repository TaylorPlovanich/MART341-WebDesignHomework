Delicious Banana Bread Recipe

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavor. Friends and family say it's by far the best!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 ⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Beat sugar and butter in another bowl, then add eggs and mashed bananas. Stir the wet mixture into the dry ingredients. Pour batter into the pan and bake for about 60 minutes.
