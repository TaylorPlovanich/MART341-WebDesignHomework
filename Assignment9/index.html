Banana Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 ⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and brown sugar together, then stir in eggs and mashed bananas.
  4. Combine the banana mixture with the flour mixture until just blended.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and bake for 60 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving.

Pro Tips

Use ripe bananas with brown spots for the best flavor. Store the bread properly to keep it moist.
